Reflection

The Digital Media and Web Development course has been transformative in shaping my technical and professional skills. Through structured assignments and projects, I learned the importance of meticulous planning, time management, and professional branding, reinforced by industry-standard courses and lectures. Designing my portfolio website, selecting 10 key portfolio pieces, and practicing effective networking through tailored messages strengthened both my technical proficiency and communication skills across various digital platforms, including my portfolio and LinkedIn.

This course emphasized practical tools such as Adobe digital imaging, project management, e-commerce, advanced web design, HTML, JavaScript, PHP, SEO, web analytics, and UX/UI design, reinforcing how organization and digital literacy contribute to a strong professional identity. By integrating resume and cover letter development, content mapping, and portfolio building, I obtained hands-on experience in presenting myself as a cohesive and marketable professional in the digital space.

Additionally, the program has been instrumental in helping me transform traditional health data into real-time, paperless data-sharing systems for end users, partner organizations, and decision-makers, enabling timely access to analyzed data for informed decision-making and evidence-based outbreak control. It enhanced my ability to design dashboards, integrate multiple data streams, and present actionable insights efficiently via GitHub platforms.
