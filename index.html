Reflection

The Digital Media and Web Development course has been transformative in shaping my technical and professional skills. Through structured assignments like the 2-week portfolio schedule plan and LinkedIn profile enhancement, I learned the importance of meticulous planning, time management, and professional branding. Designing my portfolio website, selecting 10 key portfolio pieces, and practicing effective networking through tailored messages strengthened both my technical proficiency and communication skills.

This course also emphasized practical tools such as Adobe Creative Cloud, Excel/Google Sheets for scheduling, and LinkedIn optimization, reinforcing how organization and digital literacy contribute to a strong professional identity. By integrating resume and cover letter development, content mapping, and portfolio building, I gained hands-on experience in presenting myself as a cohesive and marketable professional in the digital space.

Additionally, the program has been instrumental in helping me transform traditional health data into real-time, paperless data sharing systems for end users, partner organizations, and decision-makers. It enhanced my ability to design dashboards, integrate data streams, and present actionable insights efficiently.

Key Takeaways:

  • Structured planning enhances productivity and goal achievement.
  • Professional branding through a polished LinkedIn profile and portfolio website is essential.
  • Effective communication fosters meaningful professional connections.
  • Technical skills with Adobe Creative Cloud and web design tools improve portfolio quality and marketability.
  • Digital Media and Web Development enables real-time, paperless data sharing for end users, partner organizations, and decision-makers.

About Me

With over 30 years of experience, I specialize in data science, public health emergency response, GIS mapping, and epidemiological research across Africa, Asia, the Middle East, Europe, and North America. I have collaborated with WHO, UNICEF, CDC, FHI/USAID, and Save the Children. Currently, I am developing a personal website showcasing an infographics platform with three decades of information management work.
